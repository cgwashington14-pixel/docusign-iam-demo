    When IAM Essentials Is Enough
    Good fit: Simple approval chains, vendor paper AI review via Iris, embedded signing, Connect webhooks to ERP on completion.
    Add CLM when: Multi-round negotiation, clause libraries, obligation tracking, ERP pre-fill at initiation, or template generation with mandatory state clauses.
